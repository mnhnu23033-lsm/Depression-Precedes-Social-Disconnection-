Methods Summary

Key design and analytic decisions.

Data

CHARLS (China Health and Retirement Longitudinal Study), waves 2015, 2018, 2020. Nationally representative sample, adults ≥45 years.

Sample Selection

Coarsened exact matching (CEM) on age, sex, education, residence. Final n=4,813 (1,966 with / 2,847 without functional limitations). Survey weights applied.

Statistical Model

Cross-lagged panel models (CLPM) in Mplus 8.3. Estimator: WLSMV with Theta parameterisation. Multi-group analysis by functional limitation status.

Measures

Loneliness: CESD-10 single item. Social Isolation: 4-item index. Depressive symptoms: CESD-9. Self-rated health: 3-category. Functional limitation: ADL/IADL.
