Methods Summary

Key design and analytic decisions.

Data source

CHARLS — China Health and Retirement Longitudinal Study, waves 2015, 2018, 2020. Nationally representative, adults ≥45 years. Funded by NSFC and NIA.

Sample selection

Coarsened exact matching (CEM) on age, sex, education, residence. Final n=4,813 (1,966 with / 2,847 without functional limitations). Survey weights applied.

Statistical model

Cross-lagged panel models (CLPM) in Mplus 8.3. Estimator: WLSMV, Theta parameterisation. Multi-group DIFFTEST for functional limitation moderation.

Measures

L Loneliness (CESD-10 item) · SI Social Isolation (4-item) · D Depression (CESD-9) · H Self-rated Health (3-cat) · Functional limitation: ADL/IADL
